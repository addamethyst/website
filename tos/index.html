Privacy Policy

What data is stored?

We do not store any data, except for when you use the link command. After successfully linking your Discord account and your Minecraft account, we store your Discord User ID and your Minecraft UUID.

What is this data used for?

Your Discord User ID and your Minecraft UUID is stored together to see which Discord account is linked to which Minecraft account. The data is used so that you can execute commands without providing a username argument.

How long is this data stored?

Indefinitely, until you decide to unlink your accounts, where your data gets deleted off of our database.

How do I get my data removed?

To get your data removed from the database, simply run the unlink command in Discord.

Is this data shared with anyone?

No. No third parties are ever given access to this data.
