Amethyst

Amethyst is a bot focused on the Hypixel network, allowing you to track
players' stats on different Hypixel gamemodes and do much, much more!

Features of Amethyst
Easy to use

Amethyst is a really easy to use Hypixel bot. Being integrated with Discord's slash commands, all you need to do is input the command's name and let Discord take the rest.

Stats, quickly

Simple to see stats, allowing you to quickly check and track stats of you or other people.

Most gamemodes

Amethyst has stats of most Hypixel gamemodes, with more added with updates frequently.
